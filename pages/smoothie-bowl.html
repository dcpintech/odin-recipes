Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Description

Here's a quick and easy smoothie bowl recipe. This recipe only requires 5 ingredients and is ready to serve in less than 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 300 g Frozen Fruits
  • 350 ml Milk
  • Granola
  • Chia Seeds
  • Assorted Fruit

Steps

  1. Pour frozen fruit and milk into blender
  2. Blend on low speed
  3. Pour into bowl
  4. Top with chia seeds and fruits of your choice
  5. Serve immediatly
