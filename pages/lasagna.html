Lasagna Recipe

Description

Looking for a italian delicassy that will impress your friends at your next get together? Then don't hesitate to try out this amazing Lasagna recipe.

This recipe is quick to make and packed with flavor. After your first try you won't even think about making lasagna any other way. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Assorted Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and assorted vegetables on a stove.
  2. Bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a boil and cook the pasta sheets for 8-10 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 F/190 C
  4. Assemble Lasagna by spreading a layer of meat and vegetable sauce at the bottom and top with pasta sheets. Then top the pasta sheets with your choice of cheeses. Repeat these layers until done and top the everything with the remaining cheeses.
  5. Cover tray with foil
  6. Bake for 25 minutes
  7. Remove foil and cook for an additional 25 minutes
  8. Wait 15 minutes and serve your lasagna
