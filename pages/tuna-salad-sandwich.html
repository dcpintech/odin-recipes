Tuna Salad Sandwich Recipe

Description

I never get tired of an excellent tuna salad recipe, and of the hundreds of versions Iâ€™ve made, this version is the best. Serve on toast, crackers, a bed of greens, or grilled as a very delicious tuna melt.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 2 Cooked eggs
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Pour tuna and mayonnaise into a bowl
  2. Cut up pickles and eggs and pour add them to the bowl
  3. Use a fork to breakdown the tuna
  4. Mix until the entire bowl is homogeneous
  5. Spread mixture on two slices of whole wheat bread
  6. Add two more slices to make a sandwich
  7. Serve immediatly or store for later use(this recipe lasts for 24 hours in the fridge)
